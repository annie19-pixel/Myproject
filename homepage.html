Wildlife in Kenya

An inredible 25% of the world's biodiversity is found in Africa-and Kenya, on the eastern
coast of the African Continent, is one of the ten most biodiverse countries on earth.

According to recent estimates, Kenya is home to 25,00 animal species, including many
large mammals, 7,000 plant species, and 2,000 fungi and bacteria species.These species
thrive in Kenya's rich array of ecosystems, from lush forests and savannahs to the snow-capped
peaks of Mount Kenya and arid scrublands. There are also plentof aquatic
hanitats in Kenya, both along the coast and inland. Kenya is home to 467 inland lake and
