White-bellied go-away birds

The white-bellied go-away bird has a call that sounds like'go-away'.It has grey
plumage, a white belly, and a huge crest on top of its head. It also has white marks on
its tail, which are visible when the bird is in flight.

White-bellied go-away birds live in the arid savannahs and woodlands of Kenya,
Tanzania, Uganda, Sudan, South Sudan, Somalia and Ethiopia.The IUCN lists this bird
as leats concern, with no major threats to its survival.
